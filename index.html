    Summary about Mushaf

    • Getting started

      The name of the novel “MUSHAF” (Arabic name of the Holy Quran) gives us a hint that it is a story related to religious content or it might contain some verses with beautiful meaning and deep tafseer.

    • Spiritual Revelation Unveiled

      Yes it is connected with all these aspects with such beauty and intellect that one might have never thought of Quran that way.

    • Reader of This Novel Journey

      A reader of this novel journeys through suspense, excitement, love, trust, betrayal, crisis and harsh realities of a brutal life. Despite having all this, this novel leaves a permanent place in the reader’s heart.

    • Morning's Enigmatic Encounter

      At a morning bus stand, Mehmal encounters a mysterious black-skinned girl with a captivating aura and a peculiar black-covered book. Nimra Ahmed skillfully weaves a tale of curiosity, magic, and the profound impact of a chance meeting on Mehmal's world.

    • Inherited Turmoil Unveiled

      The novel centers on Mehmal Ibraheem, a discontented girl living with her mother, Musarat, in her late father's house, contested by his family. With striking beauty and a quick temper, Mehmal grapples with the family's oppressive behavior, resenting their claim to her father's property.

    • Spiritual Liberation Unveiled

      Mehmal returns the Mushaf to the mysterious girl, facing betrayal as Fuwaad sells her to Hamayun. Locked and desperate, she escapes to a madrassa roof, discovering Hamayun is an undercover cop. The novel beautifully unfolds as the Quran's verses guide Mehmal through darkness, offering solace and answers in every situation.

    • Sacred Transformation Unveiled

      After this unusual event the life of Mehmal Ibraheem changes, she starts to cover her head, reads Mushaf and tries to understand the meaning of the holy verses of the Holy Quran. Quran starts to talk with her as it is called Kalaam Paak and Kalaam in Arabic is talk. Whatever she thinks, is answered in the Quran.

    • Farishtay's Deceptive Turn

      Initially a mysterious ally in the madrassa, Farishtay, revealed as Mehmal's sister, provides crucial support in understanding life. Despite the initial admiration, the character takes a surprising turn, evoking hatred by the novel's end.

    • Quranic Reflections Unveiled

      Nimra Ahmed intertwines life's trials with Quranic verses, highlighting the Quran as a living miracle, not mere ancient tales. This novel transforms perspectives, emphasizing the timeless relevance of the Quran in the present age.

    • Divine Trust: Lessons Learned

      Trusting Allah alone, seeking guidance, and recognizing even the inherently good can be misled. This novel underscores that practicing Islam is a lifelong journey, with constant tests for everyone, regardless of their righteousness.

    • Life-Changing Novel Recommendation

      I recommend you this novel and I am sure even if it didn’t change your life it would definitely leave you in great awe. In every incident of this novel is a hidden message. May Allah grant us the noor and insight to actually understand the treasure of each verse revealed by Allah.

    • Learning Paths

      We might not be Mehmal but we can actually learn a lot from her.

    The Author

    Nimra Ahmed

    Style: Art, Fiction

    Nemrah Niazi (pen name: Nemrah Ahmed) is a Pakistani Urdu language novelist and author. She was born on 9 September 1990 in Bhakkar, Pakistan. She is originally from Mianwali. Nimra Ahmed is a contemporary fiction writer. Her novel’s main themes are spirituality, romanticism, family problems, violence, and corruption. She rose to prominence through her novels Jannat Ke Patte, Namal, Mushaf, and Mala.
